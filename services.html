Our Services

These are the core services I offer through WebOps Studio. Each is built entirely from scratch — no templates, no shortcuts — and tailored to meet real client needs with clean, responsive, high-performance code.

Custom Website Design

What it is:

A fully custom, multi-page website built from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Why clients need it:

Most templated websites are bloated, slow, or generic. You need a site that reflects your brand, works across devices, and loads fast — without relying on someone else's design.

How my approach is different:

I build every line of code by hand, optimizing for speed, structure, and simplicity. This means faster load times, less breakage, and easier long-term maintenance.

Client value:

A clean, one-of-a-kind website that looks sharp, runs fast, and stands out — because it's built for you, not mass-market use.

Landing Pages

What it is:

A high-converting, single-page website focused on one goal: getting users to take action (sign up, buy, donate, etc.).

Why clients need it:

Landing pages are crucial for campaigns, launches, or services that need targeted messaging. But a slow or cluttered landing page can kill conversions.

How my approach is different:

I design every layout and element with intention — fast-loading code, zero distractions, mobile-first structure, and a laser focus on the call-to-action.

Client value:

Higher engagement, more signups/sales, and full control over messaging — all without the noise of bloated platforms.

Website Redesign & Cleanup

What it is:

A full front-end refresh of your existing website, focusing on layout, usability, and performance.

Why clients need it:

If your current site looks outdated, loads slowly, or breaks on mobile — you’re losing visitors (and sales) every day.

How my approach is different:

Instead of patching over bad code, I rebuild it from the ground up using clean, modern standards. I audit your current site, identify pain points, and rebuild only what’s necessary.

Client value:

A faster, better-looking, more responsive version of your current site — one that users actually enjoy using.

Mobile Optimization

What it is:

Custom restructuring and styling of your site to look and perform perfectly on all mobile devices.

Why clients need it:

Over 60% of web traffic is mobile — and if your site doesn’t work well on phones, you're losing most of your audience.

How my approach is different:

I code responsive breakpoints and adaptive layouts by hand — not relying on generic framework classes — so your site scales naturally across screen sizes.

Client value:

A mobile-first experience that improves accessibility, speed, and engagement — especially for users on the go.

Contact & Form Integration

What it is:

Custom-coded contact forms, message capture forms, and functional front-end integrations for user outreach.

Why clients need it:

You need more than just an email address on a page — forms create trust, streamline communication, and make it easier for clients or customers to reach you.

How my approach is different:

I design and code forms manually, ensuring they match your design, validate input properly, and can be wired up with your email or backend.

Client value:

A professional, working contact system that feels like part of your brand — not a clunky afterthought.

Front-End Refactoring

What it is:

An audit and cleanup of your current site’s front-end code, focusing on structure, performance, and scalability.

Why clients need it:

Over time, many sites become bloated and buggy. Refactoring is like tuning your engine — it keeps things clean, lean, and future-ready.

How my approach is different:

I don't just rearrange code — I strip out what you don't need, re-organize what you do, and rebuild logic in a way that makes sense. All done without breaking your current visual design.

Client value:

A leaner, faster site that’s easier to maintain, more SEO-friendly, and future-proofed for growth.
