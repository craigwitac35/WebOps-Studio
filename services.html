The Pitch

Why hand-coded
beats a template.

Squarespace and Wix are fast to set up and slow to defend. You're renting a site, not owning one. When the platform changes, your site changes. When the plugin breaks, your site breaks. When you stop paying, your site disappears.

Hand-coded sites load faster, rank better, and hold up longer — because there's nothing in them that doesn't need to be there. You get clean code, a real handoff, and a site that belongs to you.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do you work with clients outside Minnesota?

Yes. Most of the work happens remotely anyway. Location doesn't matter as long as communication is clear.

How long does a build take?

A landing page typically takes 1–2 weeks. A small business site runs 2–4 weeks. Larger builds are scoped individually. Timeline depends on how quickly content and feedback come in.

What do I need to provide?

Your logo, any photos you want to use, and a clear idea of what the site needs to do. I'll handle structure, copy direction, and all the code.

Will I be able to update the site myself?

Yes. The code is clean and straightforward. If you're comfortable with basic HTML, you can make text and image updates yourself. The maintenance retainer covers ongoing updates if you'd prefer.

Why not just use Squarespace or Wix?

You can. But you'll pay monthly forever, deal with platform updates you didn't ask for, and end up with a site that looks like every other site on that template. A hand-coded site is faster, owned outright, and built specifically for you.
