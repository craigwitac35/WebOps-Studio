Guardian 4 Heroes

Client Guardian 4 Heroes
Sector Nonprofit, Equine Therapy
Built With HTML5 · CSS3 · Vanilla JS
Live Site guardian4heroes.org ↗
Live Site

The Mission

Guardian 4 Heroes provides equine therapy to veterans and first responders to support mental and physical well-being. They had no website and needed something custom-built, fully responsive, and emotionally resonant, something that could communicate their values while being practical and easy to navigate.

My Role

  • Visual design, imagery, layout, typography
  • Full front-end build from scratch
  • Custom mobile and tablet layouts
  • Contact form wired to email
  • Donation page with tiered buttons
  • Accessible, high-contrast design

Technical Breakdown

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, no frameworks
  • Hosted on GitHub Pages, migrated to live domain
  • Lightweight, fast load, no unnecessary scripts
  • Mobile-first responsive breakpoints

The Outcome

A warm, mission-aligned site that matched their identity and served their goals. Clear donation pathways, a powerful hero section, and a clean mobile layout that scales beautifully on all devices. The organization was thrilled, and their donors and supporters have noticed.

"Craig went above and beyond. He didn't just build us a website, he brought our mission to life. We get compliments on it constantly."

— Guardian 4 Heroes Team
