Full-Stack Project

Mission Possible: Hooves & Paws

A complete event registration platform for a Minnesota nonprofit fundraiser benefiting equine therapy and K9 training programs for veterans and first responders.

The organizers needed more than a marketing site – they needed a full system to manage registrations, track payments, and run a real event. Everything built from scratch.

What was built: cinematic homepage with hand-composited logo; events page with day-of details and pricing; multi-participant registration form with t-shirt sizing and dynamic pricing rules; unique reference codes tied to Givebutter; confirmation page with copy-to-clipboard codes; private admin dashboard with live stats, search, and shirt-order reporting.
