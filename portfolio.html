Guardian 4 Heroes

Sector Nonprofit, Equine Therapy

Live Site guardian4heroes.org ↗
Live Site

The Mission

Guardian 4 Heroes provides equine therapy to veterans and first responders. They needed a site that felt mission-aligned, emotionally grounded, and easy to navigate.

My Role

  • Visual design, layout, typography
  • Full front-end build from scratch
  • Mobile and tablet breakpoints
  • Contact form wired to email
  • Donation page with tiered buttons
  • Accessible, high-contrast design

Technical Breakdown

  • Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript (no frameworks)
  • Hosted on GitHub Pages, migrated to live domain
  • Lightweight and fast with minimal scripts
  • Mobile-first structure with clean hierarchy

The Outcome

A warm, mission-aligned site with clear donation pathways and a strong mobile experience. The organization was thrilled and supporters regularly compliment the build.

“Craig went above and beyond. He did not just build us a website, he brought our mission to life. We get compliments on it constantly.” Guardian 4 Heroes Team
