01 Featured Work
Full-Stack Project

Mission Possible: Hooves & Paws

A complete event registration platform for a Minnesota nonprofit fundraiser benefiting equine therapy and K9 training programs for veterans and first responders.

The organizers needed more than a marketing site – they needed a full system to manage registrations, track payments, and run a real event. Everything was built from scratch.

What was built: a cinematic homepage with hand-composited logo; an events page with day-of details and pricing; a multi-participant registration form with t-shirt sizing and dynamic pricing rules; unique reference codes tied to Givebutter payment integration; a confirmation page with copy-to-clipboard reference codes; and a private admin dashboard with live stats, search, and shirt-order reporting.
