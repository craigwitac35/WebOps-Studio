Guardian 4 Heroes

Guardian 4 Heroes is an equine therapy nonprofit serving veterans and first responders — people I have a deep connection with as a veteran myself. They needed a site that felt emotionally grounded, mission-driven, and easy to navigate.

I built everything from scratch in HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, keeping it lightweight, accessible, and conversion-focused. The design prioritizes clear donation pathways and trust-building imagery without relying on heavy frameworks.
