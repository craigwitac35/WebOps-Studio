The Hidden Risk

Storm Damage Isn't Always Visible.

A roof can look fine from the ground while hidden damage spreads. A quick inspection now can prevent leaks and expensive interior repairs later.

  • Missing or lifted shingles
  • Hail bruising and granule loss
  • Flashing damage around vents and chimneys
  • Hidden leaks that show up weeks later
  • Insurance paperwork confusion and delays
