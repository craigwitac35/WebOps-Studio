ABOUT
THE PROCESS

A clear process built on discipline, structure, and clean code.

WebOps Studio is led by Craig Baumann, a U.S. Army veteran and self-taught front-end developer based in Minnesota. What began as curiosity became a focused approach to building websites with structure, clarity, and strong fundamentals.

The mission is simple: build custom websites with precision, discipline, and attention to detail.

My Story

Built Through Discipline

I did not take a traditional path through design school or a CS degree. I learned by building, testing, and refining real projects from scratch.

After the Army, that same mindset carried into web development: disciplined execution, adaptability, and focus on the mission.

WebOps Studio is the result: hand-coded websites built with care, clarity, and long-term maintainability.
