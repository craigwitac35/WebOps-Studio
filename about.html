I'm Craig Baumann, a U.S. Army veteran and self-taught front-end developer based in Minnesota. I build websites the same way I approach everything that matters: with discipline, precision, and zero interest in shortcuts.

My Story

No Roadmap. Just Grit.

I did not come up through design school or a traditional developer pipeline. My path was built through trial and error, curiosity-fueled late nights, and a stubborn need to understand how things actually work.

I would break code just to see what happened, rebuild it, and then push it further. It started as experimentation, but over time it became a craft. No roadmap. Just grit, repetition, and real progress.

After the Army, I brought that same mindset into web development. The discipline, adaptability, and mission-oriented focus carried over naturally. WebOps Studio became the name that pulled all of it together: a brand built to represent clean execution, thoughtful design, and hand-coded work that actually means something.
