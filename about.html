About

Veteran. Developer.
Builder.

I'm Craig Baumann — U.S. Army veteran turned self-taught front-end developer based in Minnesota. I build websites the way I approached every mission: with discipline, precision, and a refusal to cut corners.

My Story

No Roadmap. Just Grit.

I didn't come up through design school. My path was trial and error, curiosity-fueled late nights, and a determination to figure things out from scratch. I'd break code just to see what happened. Tinkered like it was a puzzle. No roadmap, just grit.

After the Army, I brought that same mindset to web development. The discipline, the adaptability, the mission-oriented focus, all of it translated directly. Eventually things clicked. And then kept clicking.

WebOps Studio became the name that wrapped it all together. A brand built to showcase what I can do, not just with code, but with care. Every page you see here was made by hand.
